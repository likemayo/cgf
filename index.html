About China Gulf Forum

The China-Gulf Forum was launched in 2019 at New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) and is the first student-organized conference on China-Gulf relations in the United Arab Emirates. Over the past three iterations, China-Gulf Forum has accumulated over 1000 attendees from all over the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to address key aspects of the multilateral relationship between China and the Gulf region, with a target audience of students and young professionals, by bringing scholars, industry leaders, and government practitioners to share and discuss their expertise in the two regions.
